Rules used by this checker

1. Same block = clashA student cannot take two subjects from the same block because they are timetabled at the same time.
2. Pink blocks are unavailableBlock A and Block C are marked as not available to BTEC students in the source grid.
3. DP options for CP are D, E and FThe highlighted options on the right of the source grid are DP Block D, DP Block E and DP Block F.
4. Core/BTEC items are listed separatelyBTEC Business, PPS, CE, MOE Arabic, Language and Cultural Studies, Islamic Studies / Study Hall are shown for reference, but the checker focuses on subject combinations.
